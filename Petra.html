Petra

  • Location: Southern Jordan, near the town of Wadi Musa.
  • Nickname: Known as the "Rose City" due to the pink-hued sandstone.
  • Historical Significance: Served as the capital of the Nabatean Kingdom around 4th century BCE.
  • Architecture: Features rock-cut architecture, tombs, and temples.
  • Highlights: The Treasury (Al-Khazneh), the Monastery, and the Siq canyon.
  • UNESCO Status: Designated a World Heritage Site in 1985.
